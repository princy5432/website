Climate of India

India experiences a wide range of weather conditions. An year is majorly divided into four seasons.

It begins from mid-November and stays till February in Northern India.

The Winter season

It begins from mid-November and stays till February in Northern India.

The Summer season

It begins from March to the mid of June. Temperature is recorded maximum during the months of May and June. Schools also have summer vacations during this time.

The Rainy season

It begins from mid-June to September.

The Autumn season

It begins after the rainy season from October to middle of December.
