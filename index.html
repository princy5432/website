Chocolate Milkshake Recipe

Image by Steve Buissinne from Pixabay

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup full cream milk
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • Ice cubes

PROCEDURE

  1. Add milk in a blender.
  2. Also add cocoa powder and sugar.
  3. Add vanilla ice cream and ice cubes.
  4. Blend well to form frothy milkshake.
  5. Pour the prepared milkshake into a glass.
  6. Optionally, you can add some chopped chocolate on top.
